Yes, our basic file conversion service is completely free with no hidden charges. We offer optional premium features for power users, but most users find our free tool meets all their needs.

We support all major file formats including PDF, DOCX, XLSX, PPTX, JPG, PNG, TXT, and more. If you have a question about a specific format, please contact us.
